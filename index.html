Changing Colors

Cute Flexi Maker lets you change the color of every element so your creation looks exactly the way you want.

  1. Skin color changes the base color for the entire flexi.
  2. To open the color menu, click the colored circle to the right of the category name.
  3. For elements with multiple components, click the drop-down arrow to view all sub-components for that element.
  4. Don't see the perfect color? Click the plus and select a custom color!
